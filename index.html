Research

I'm interested in the kind of statistics that has the small and beautiful ideas, but runs perfectly well at large-scale.

My research interest covers:

  • Statistical machine learning, theory and methodology:
    • High-dimensional linear models and variable selection
    • Supervised distance metric learning and dimensionality reduction
  • Statistical machine learning, applications in biomedicine:
    • High-dimensional data integration and network inference
    • Systems genetics and translational bioinformatics
Programming
